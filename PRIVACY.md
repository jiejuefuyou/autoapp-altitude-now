# Privacy Policy

**AltitudeNow** — last updated 2026-04-29

## What we collect

**Nothing.** AltitudeNow does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, makes no network requests, includes no analytics SDKs, and includes no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## Sensor data

AltitudeNow reads the device's built-in barometric pressure sensor (`CMAltimeter`) to compute relative altitude. This sensor data is processed entirely on-device and stored only locally in the app's sandboxed Documents directory. It is never transmitted off the device. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. AltitudeNow receives only the entitlement status (purchased / not purchased). Apple's own privacy policy governs purchase information: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## Children

AltitudeNow is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any data, so no data about children is ever processed.
