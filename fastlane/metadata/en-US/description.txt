A simple, honest altimeter for iPhone.

AltitudeNow reads your iPhone's built-in barometric pressure sensor (every iPhone since the iPhone 6 has one) and shows you live altitude and pressure. Tap Start to begin recording a session — see your elevation gain and loss as a real-time chart, then save it for later.

WHAT IT DOES
• Live relative altitude — the distance up or down from where you started
• Live atmospheric pressure (hPa or inHg)
• Real-time line chart that grows as you move
• Persistent session log with min, max, gain, and average pressure
• Custom calibration offset to align with a known reference altitude (Premium)

NO GPS. NO INTERNET. NO TRACKING.
• AltitudeNow does not request your location and does not need GPS
• Zero network traffic. Zero analytics. Zero advertising SDKs.
• Sensor data and session history live only in the app's sandbox
• Delete the app, delete the data — there is no cloud copy

WHO IT'S FOR
• Hikers checking elevation gain on a trail
• Cyclists profiling a climb
• Pilots and skydivers wanting a quick reference
• Anyone curious about the air pressure at their location

PREMIUM (one-time $2.99)
• Unlimited session history (free tier keeps the most recent only)
• Custom calibration offset
• Imperial/metric and hPa/inHg unit toggle
• CSV export of any session
• Session detail charts

No subscription. Pay once, keep it forever.

REQUIREMENTS
iOS 17 or later. The barometric sensor is part of the iPhone hardware (iPhone 6 and newer); it is not present in the iOS Simulator.
